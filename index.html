How to use
  • Hit 'Use random quote', or
  • Enter your own text above and hit 'Use my own text'
  • Start typing, and the displayed text will turn green/red based on the correctness of your input
Known limitations
  • Lit Ipsum API for the random quotes does not allow repeated requests within a short timeframe. If you need a new quote, you have to wait for ~5 seconds
  • Although there is a sanitization logic for some special characters like double quotes, you might still encounter other characters which you can't replicate in your keyboard. You'll have to copy-paste them or request a new text. The best is to report them here (or create a pull request) so we can include them during sanitization.
  • New lines are also a bit bumpy. The current comparison isn't smart enough to match them, so keep that in mind and avoid them whenever you can.
  • Sorry that the app does not congratulate you in any way, or give you your typing speed. Maybe one day...
